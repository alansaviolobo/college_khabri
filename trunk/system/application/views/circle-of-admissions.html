      Note : For institute quota there is no centralized admission procedure, candidate has to contact college principal to understand Institute quota admission procedure. For Institute quota you have to apply to each college separately, as there are more than 200 engineering colleges, it is not possible to apply to each college. So you need to find a college that offers stream of your interest and understand college profile like placement record, facylty profile, fee structure, popularity rank, etc to shortlist few colleges where you can apply for Institute quota. College profiles are available at collegekhabri.com.
